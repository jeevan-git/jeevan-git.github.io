<div style="text-align: left">
  MechTRIX, the national mechanical engineering exhibition has been taking the steps towards the growth of students, in every way possible. Every year,a new concept, sow it among the students and faculties, nurture it and share it with the world.MechTRIX 2020 operated under the theme “ For a Better Tomorrow ” promoting the culture of waste management and environmental accountability. Various events that included students from different schools, colleges and universities has based on this theme.The main motto of this exhibition is not only to provide a platform for students,who are the building blocks of the country, to display their skills but also to make them aware of environmental challenges.
